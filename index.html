Welcome to the CCT Portal

The purpose of the portal is to provide users with a central location in which to access information pertaining to Slumbering Admins,
including information regarding the admins' current state status and the date/time the machine was last started/stopped, and more.

Users will have the ability to query current members of the Slumbering Admin program and to modify the membership status of the admins.

This portal is also the future home of Site Deployments.
